AMS Password Reset and Errors

Software üìÖ Last Updated: December 17, 2024

Comprehensive guide for resolving AMS (Application Management System) password issues and common login errors.

Overview

This article covers the most common AMS password-related issues, including password resets, account lockouts, and authentication errors. AMS is a critical system for application management, and quick resolution of access issues is essential for maintaining productivity.

üí° Tip: Keep your AMS password secure and follow the password complexity requirements to avoid lockouts.

How to Reset Your AMS Password

1 Access the AMS Login Page

Navigate to the AMS portal URL provided by your organization. Click on the "Forgot Password?" link below the login form.

2 Enter Your Username or Email

Provide your registered AMS username or email address. Ensure the information is entered correctly to receive the reset instructions.

3 Verify Your Identity

Complete the verification process by entering the code sent to your registered email or phone number. Check your spam folder if you don't receive the code within 5 minutes.

4 Create a New Password

Enter a new password that meets the AMS password requirements (see below). Confirm the password by entering it again in the confirmation field.

5 Complete the Reset

Click "Reset Password" and wait for confirmation. You should receive a success message and can now log in with your new password.

Password Requirements

Your AMS password must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum of 12 characters in length
  • At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
  • At least one number (0-9)
  • At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
  • Cannot contain your username or email address
  • Cannot be one of your last 5 passwords

Common AMS Errors and Solutions

Error: "Invalid Credentials"

Cause: Incorrect username or password entered.

Solution:

  1. Verify that Caps Lock is turned off
  2. Ensure you're using the correct username (not your email)
  3. Try resetting your password using the steps above
  4. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again

Error: "Account Locked"

Cause: Multiple failed login attempts detected.

Solution:

  1. Wait 30 minutes for the automatic unlock
  2. Or contact the Service Desk for immediate unlock
  3. Reset your password after the account is unlocked

Error: "Session Expired"

Cause: Your AMS session has timed out due to inactivity.

Solution:

  1. Close all AMS browser windows
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Open a new browser window and log in again
  4. Save your work regularly to prevent data loss

Error: "Password Expired"

Cause: AMS passwords expire every 90 days for security purposes.

Solution:

  1. Click "Change Password Now" when prompted
  2. Enter your current password
  3. Create a new password following the requirements
  4. Confirm and save the new password
üí° Best Practice: Set a calendar reminder to change your password every 85 days to avoid unexpected lockouts.

Unable to Receive Reset Email

If you're not receiving the password reset email:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Verify that your email address is correct in the system
  • Add ams-noreply@company.com to your safe senders list
  • Wait 10 minutes and request a new reset email
  • Contact the Service Desk if the issue persists

Security Best Practices

  • Never share your password: Keep your AMS credentials confidential
  • Use a password manager: Store complex passwords securely
  • Enable MFA: Add multi-factor authentication if available
  • Log out properly: Always sign out when finishing your session
  • Report suspicious activity: Contact IT immediately if you suspect unauthorized access

When to Contact Support

Reach out to the Service Desk if you experience:

  • Repeated password reset failures
  • Cannot access your registered email
  • Account locked for more than 30 minutes
  • Errors not covered in this guide
  • Suspected security breach or unauthorized access

Summary

AMS password issues can be quickly resolved by following the self-service reset process. Remember to create strong, unique passwords that meet all requirements, and contact the Service Desk if you encounter persistent problems or need immediate assistance with account access.
